PCI Compliance

E-xact Transactions Ltd. has achieved full compliance with the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS) each year since it was first mandated to service providers in 2004 (then known as CISP).

E-xact’s continued PCI Compliance assures that merchant and cardholder data is protected by the industry’s highest security standards.

For more details about E-xact's status, please visit the PCI section of the Visa website and view our entry on the official list of providers.
